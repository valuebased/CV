Joy Oghenemano

Founder/CEO of Valuebased Technology and Tech 4 the Teen Girl .

Joy E. Oghenemano is a creative, innovative, ambitious, self-motivated lady with a decade of successful experience in educating and training especially young teenagers in the area of IT. Joy earned a degree in BSc Computer Science in 2011 from the University of Port Harcourt. Since then, she has pursued her passion for Female development and training especially in the area of ICT. Her Recent activity as a Pioneer of the Tech for the Teen Girls program which is one the program set up by Value based Technology, has impacted over 500 girls in various schools and religious bodies across the nation Nigeria. The program has also help to bridge the Gap between Female World and male dominated IT World.

Joy has also pioneered and work on other programs which involves IT Consultation, development and management of website and school management system for various education institution across the country. She is currently holding the position of a director in Blessed International School and Value Based Technology.
